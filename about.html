Our story

Born from a love of gathering people

EventEase was founded on a simple but powerful belief — that every individual should be able to cultivate their own outstanding event regardless of their social pull, experience, or connections. Whether an intimate dinner for twelve or a wedding for five hundred, all who wish to celebrate should have the platform to do so.

We began as a small team with big dreams, and have since grown into a family of six specialists who collectively cover every detail from initial concept to final farewell. We are planners, stylists, logistics experts, and storytellers — and we love what we do.

Our work spans corporate galas, intimate celebrations, destination weddings, product launches, and cultural events. Based in South Africa, we bring a distinctly local warmth and creativity to every occasion.
